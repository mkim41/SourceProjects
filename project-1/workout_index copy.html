  1. Get changed into the most comfortable clothes
  2. Pack a water bottle, keys, and earphones
  3. Head out to the nearest gym
  4. Find a space at the gym where you can stretch, then follow a 5 minute stretching video on YouTube
  5. After stretching, sanitize and wipe a treadmill
  6. Use your earphones to listen to a podcast or songs, or watch youtube videos for distraction as needed
  7. Start to walk on the treadmill slowly for up to 5 minutes
  8. Start to walk faster for 10 minutes, faster than 4.0 speed
  9. Drink some water
  10. Run/jog for a minute approximately at 7.0 speed
  11. Walk again for 10 minutes, faster than 4.0 speed
  12. Drink some water
  13. Run/jog again for a minute approximately at 7.0 speed
  14. Walk slowly again for up to 5 minutes
  15. Drink some water
  16. Stop the treadmill
  17. Sanitize the treadmill
  18. Head back home
